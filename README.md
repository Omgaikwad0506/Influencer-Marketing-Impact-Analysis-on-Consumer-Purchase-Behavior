# 📊 Impact of Influencer Marketing on Consumer Purchase Decision

## 📌 Overview

This project is a comprehensive market research study conducted as part of the Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS) program under the University of Mumbai.

The study focuses on analyzing how influencer marketing affects consumer buying behavior, brand perception, and purchase decisions in the digital era.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* To understand the concept and growth of influencer marketing
* To analyze its impact on consumer purchase decisions
* To examine the role of trust, credibility, and authenticity
* To study consumer perception towards influencer content
* To identify ethical issues in influencer marketing

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts Covered

* Digital Marketing
* Influencer Marketing
* Consumer Behavior
* Purchase Decision Process
* Social Media Marketing
* Consumer Trust & Engagement

---

## 📊 Research Methodology

### 📌 Data Collection

* **Primary Data:** Online survey (Google Forms)
* **Secondary Data:** Books, research papers, articles

### 📌 Sample Details

* Sample Size: 100+ respondents
* Diverse age groups, income levels, and education backgrounds

### 📌 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel (Data Analysis & Charts)

---

## 📈 Key Findings

* Instagram and YouTube are the most influential platforms
* Majority of users spend **1–4 hours daily** on social media
* Influencers significantly help in **brand discovery**
* Trust and authenticity are the most important factors
* Influencer reviews strongly impact **brand perception**

---

## 💡 Insights

* Micro & relatable influencers create higher trust
* Consumers prefer **authentic and transparent content**
* Influencer marketing acts as **modern word-of-mouth**
* High engagement leads to higher purchase intention

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

* Limited geographical area
* Small sample size
* Based on self-reported data
* Changing trends in digital marketing
